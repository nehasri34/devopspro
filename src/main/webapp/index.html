Common Plant Diseases

Tomato Leaf Spot

A fungal disease that causes dark spots on leaves and reduces crop production.

Rice Blast

Rice blast is one of the most damaging diseases affecting rice cultivation.

Wheat Rust

Wheat rust reduces grain quality and spreads rapidly during humid conditions.

Plant Protection Tips

  • 🌿 Use disease-resistant seeds.
  • 💧 Water plants regularly.
  • 🍀 Apply organic fertilizers.
  • 🪴 Remove infected leaves immediately.
  • 🐞 Monitor crops regularly for pests.
